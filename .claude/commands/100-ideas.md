# 100 Ideas

OK so now I want you to come up with your top 10 most brilliant ideas for adding extremely powerful and cool functionality that will make this system far more compelling, useful, intuitive, versatile, powerful, robust, reliable, etc for the users.

But I don't want you to just think of 10 ideas: I want you to seriously think hard and come up with one HUNDRED ideas and then only tell me your 10 VERY BEST and most brilliant, clever, and radically innovative and powerful ideas.

## Guidelines

- Be pragmatic - don't think of features that will be extremely hard to implement
- Consider complexity burden - is the feature worth the added complexity?
- Focus on user value - what will make the biggest difference to users?
- Think about differentiation - what would make this stand out?

## Output Format

For each of your top 10 ideas:

1. **Idea Name** - Brief catchy title
2. **Description** - What it does (2-3 sentences)
3. **User Value** - Why users would love it
4. **Implementation Complexity** - Low/Medium/High
5. **Priority Score** - 1-10 based on value/effort ratio
